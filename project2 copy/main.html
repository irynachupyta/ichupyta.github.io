I, Svitlana Rozghon, bring my talent and passion of many years as a fashion designer, sewing and craft instructor, and high end clothing manufacturer. I love finding the perfect look for you, customizing your garment, and adding accessories to make you feel resplendent on your special day,

We are different from other bridal shops because we work with you to find a gown style that you love, customize it if you want, and tailor it to your size by qualified seamstresses. We are a small business that wants to work closely with every client. Svitlana has been doing wedding alterations for many happy clients for over 20 years, the past 4 years in Cape Ann. Our gowns are made with quality materials and craftsmanship, and we guide you on what can be customized.
